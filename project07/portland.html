Portland, Oregon

The Airways Mechanical District is a fictional place inspired by the city of Portland, Oregon. It is a bustling metropolis of industry and technology, filled with towering skyscrapers and a bustling population. The city is home to a wide variety of businesses, from tech startups to manufacturing plants. The city is also a hub for air travel, with its many airports and airfields. The city is full of life, with its vibrant nightlife, art galleries, and cultural events. The Airways Mechanical District is a place where creativity and innovation thrive, and where the possibilities are endless.
