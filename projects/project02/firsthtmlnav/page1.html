About Captains Crew: The Animated Series

Captains Crew is a thrilling 3D animated show that follows the
adventures of F1dg3t Sheldon Bass, a half mechanical human
who is abducted to Airways School of Avians. After being
attacked by a SmokeBoi sent by the main villain, Volstarr,
F1dg3t must team up with other future heroes to defeat Volstarr
and protect the city from his army. This action-packed show
combines cutting-edge animation with an exciting story that will
keep you on the edge of your seat. Join F1dg3t and the Captains
Crew as they embark on a journey to save the city and become the
heroes they were always meant to be.
