Who is F1dg3t?

Tajrian Rahman, who goes by F1dg3t is a talented
artist who excels in converting 2D
illustrations into stunning 3D models. With a focus on
characters, environments, and objects, F1dg3t breathes
new life into flat designs by adding depth and dimension.
He honed his skills over six years, starting in high school
and has now reached a level of mastery where he is confident
in offering his 3D modeling services to clients.
