Sweden

The City of Airways is roughly based within Sweden, placing the large central hub above the lake, and monorails above the waterways. Each district is independent from the hub, but the heart of the city still stands. Sweden has long been a source of inspiration for many writers and artists, and the fictional place of Airways is no exception. Set in a world of fantasy and adventure, Airways is a place where the beauty of Swedish nature and culture is celebrated. The rolling hills, lush forests, and crystal clear lakes of Sweden are all reflected in the landscape of Airways, while the vibrant cities and small towns of Sweden provide the backdrop for the bustling city of Airways. The culture of Sweden is also reflected in the customs and traditions of the people of Airways, with traditional Swedish music, art, and cuisine all being celebrated. Airways is a place where the beauty of the Swedish landscape and culture can be experienced and enjoyed by all.
