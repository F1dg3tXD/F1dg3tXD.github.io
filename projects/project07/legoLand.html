LEGOLAND California Resort

The Airways Bloks District is a vibrant and exciting area inspired by the LEGOLAND California Resort. This district is filled with fun activities and attractions that everyone can enjoy. The area is made up of colorful buildings and streets, each designed with a unique LEGO-style architecture. Visitors can explore the area and find a variety of shops, restaurants, and entertainment venues. The district also features an amusement park with rides and attractions, as well as a water park with slides, pools, and other water activities. There is also a theater, where visitors can watch shows and movies. The Airways Bloks District is the perfect place for families and friends to have a fun and memorable time.
